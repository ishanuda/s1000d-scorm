      2. NOTE

      The in-process leak test shall be conducted using purge gas.

      A low pressure gas purge is used to protect an activated catalysts bed from exposure to air. Purge gas flow needs only to be high enough to prevent air from flowing back into the Byproduct Management Plant catalyst bed.

      If the blower motor housing is to remain open for any length of time, place a temporary cover over the opening to maintain system cleanliness.

      Prior to starting the blower motor removal, a proper container should be established to store any waste generated in accordance with local instructions.

      Non-metallic waste (packings, O-rings, etc.) shall be containerized for shore disposal. Additional guidance may be obtained from OPNAVINST 5090. 1 and OPNAVINST 5100. 19.

      Contaminated rags/cloths (rags/cloths contaminated with a hazardous substance) shall be containerized for shore disposal. Additional guidance may be obtained in OPNAVINST 5090.1 and OPNAVINST 5100.19.
