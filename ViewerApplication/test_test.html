Preliminary Requirements

Step 1

Verify that the applicable HYDRAULIC-GAGES-UTILITY PRESS - ENG 2 or ENG 3 circuit breaker on the P61-4 panel, and the FLIGHT ENGINEERS INST PNL-LOWER-IND.L circuit breaker on the P7 are open. (see Fig 1 - Hydraulic Pressure Indicating System for component location)


Next
Step 2

Uncap the aircraft wiring connector and connect to the indicator.


Next
Step 3

Carefully slide the indicator into its position in the panel.


Next
Step 4

Secure the indicator mounting clamp by tightening the round head screw adjacent to the indicator.


Next
Step 5

Reset the circuit breakers called out in Step 1.


Next
Step 6

Perform operational check out per E3-A-32-40-0000-00A-320A-A of pressure indicator during the next engine run.
